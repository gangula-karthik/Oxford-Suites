REVIEWS FROM OUR SATISFIED GUESTS

Dan Smith

We were celebrating our friend's birthday dinner here and I have to say the food was good and exceeded my expectations! Especially their bread. Lovely ambience as well, well suited for special occasions.

Pepper Potts

Room was beautifully decorated with all amenities and more. Welcome snacks and drinks were presented in the room. Staff was very professional and took care of all our special requests. Little details for the kids like teddy bears and cakes. Including a cake for my husband whose birthday we were celebrating.

Bruce Banner

Swimming pool was a letdown, but the room was amazing and up to my standards. The bathroom is the absolute best. Big, spacious and impeccable. Room service was superb as well - I did not encounter any delays and the food was to my liking. Portions were huge as well. 10/10 would stay again.

Ronnie Tan

The experience of staying in one of the luxurious hotels in Singapore is so worth it. The ambiance feels cozy, warm and inviting. Staffs are friendly and accommodating. Simple call to the concierge will grant your request, they even gave you a complimentary cake upon arrival.

Reed Richards

This is truly a 6-star hotel. Hotel exudes class and an authentic heritage feel. What impressed me was the professionalism and hospitality of every staff - such sincerity from the bellmen, to the front office and even the F&B crew. Highly recommended!
