FACILITIES
A WONDERFUL RANGE OF FACILITIES
Known for world-class amenities and services, Oxford Suites is a 5-star hotel destination for all tourists, business travelers, workers, and vacationers. Whether you are using our business lounge for business,our pool, or our spa, you will find comfort in our facilities to ensure a relaxing and rewarding experience, a successful business trip, or vacation on your next trip or stay at OXFORD SUITES.

Pool

Designed specifically for all guests to enjoy their day by the stunning 30 metre roof top swimming pool, as well as a mini wine bar with cafe and a beautiful city view from the top. It is an excellent place to unwind after a long day of business meetings or to relieve stress. The skyline and classic architecture combine to make our opulent Oxford Suites hotel rooftop swimming pool one of the most beautiful pools in the city.

Operating time: 5am - 12am

Golf

Relax on our luxurious golf course, perfect your swing on our Oxford Suites hotel gold course, or simply enjoy watching people play golf on the gold course. Guests are welcome to play rounds on our 27-hole golf course in our open field. Come here and let our golf relieve your stress! Enjoy it alone or with your loved ones, or simply gather your business partners and talk while playing!
