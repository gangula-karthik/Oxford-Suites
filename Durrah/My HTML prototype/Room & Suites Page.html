Our Accommodations



Step into quintessential luxury at The Oxford Suites Singapore. After a long day in downtown Singapore, lounge on regal chairs and sofas under shimmering chandeliers in your suite. Refresh in a marble bathroom with hand-cut French tiles and an oversized freestanding bathtub or rest on tailored linens and bedding. Your room or suite is situated in the embassy district with discerning views of Singapore Botanic Gardens. Whether you book a classic room or our luxury Presidential Suite, wonderful accommodations await at our hotel in Singapore.

Single Room


Our Single rooms are a luxurious abode away from home,
